Ship: Precursor

Lead by the scientist Mark Dubois, the Precursor is under the flag of the company Arstir Industries.

Background

The ship left earth 2154 to get more detailed informations if life is really possible on the planet Vetoo 581d. The science crew assigned to it managed to get the habitats and a comms array operational. The last message earth received from them stated, that violent thunderstorms made communications over long periods of time impossible. Nobody has heared anything from them since then.
